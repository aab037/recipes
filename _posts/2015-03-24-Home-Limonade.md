# Homemade Limonade
-	Prep time: 1 hour
-	Cook time: 5 minutes
-	Yield: Makes 2 liters approx.

### Ingredients
#### 
-	2-3 limes
-	2 limers of water
-	sugar or honey as you like
-	3-4 mint leaves



### Directions
#### Home Limonade
- slice limes, put them to the jar

- put mint into the jar

- put sugar into the jar

- place water to the jar, mix

- place jar into frige for 1-2 hours

- enjoy your drink
